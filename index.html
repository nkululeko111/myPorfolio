About Me

Hello, I'm Nkululeko Sekwanele Zwane, a dynamic professional based in Johannesburg, South Africa. I am a
seasoned Software Engineer, Filmmaker, and Entrepreneur with a commitment to innovation and excellence.

My academic journey includes earning a Film Degree from the prestigious University of the Witwatersrand,
where I honed my storytelling skills and developed a keen artistic eye. Complementing this, I achieved a
Software Engineering Diploma at Wethinkcode, solidifying my technical expertise and passion for cutting-edge
technology.

As a Software Engineer, I specialize in a diverse set of technologies, including Java, Python, HTML, CSS,
JavaScript, Django, Bootstrap, and Flutter. My proficiency extends across the development spectrum, allowing
me to create robust and scalable solutions that meet the evolving demands of the digital landscape.

In the realm of filmmaking, I leverage my academic background and creative flair to produce compelling
visual narratives. Each project is approached with a meticulous blend of technical proficiency and artistic
vision, resulting in impactful and memorable storytelling.

Beyond my technical and creative pursuits, I am an Entrepreneur driven by a vision to make a positive impact
on the world. My journey encompasses a commitment to continuous learning, adaptability to emerging trends,
and a focus on delivering solutions that resonate with users and audiences alike.

I am passionate about the intersection of technology and storytelling, and I am dedicated to contributing
meaningfully to both fields. Whether crafting code or bringing stories to life on the screen, I thrive on
challenges and am excited about the limitless possibilities that lie ahead.

Let's connect and explore how my unique blend of skills and experiences can bring value to your projects and
endeavors.
